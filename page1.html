AML MUN

After a break of 4 years, our Highschool is bringing back the prestigious conference of AMLMUN, on the 22nd and 23rd of November 2025, in the city of Athens.
This year the theme of our conference is “Democracy, Good Governance and Civil Society”.

Even though 4 years have passed since our last MUN conference, not a lot has changed in the world. There are many problems that continue to trouble our world and many that have aroused in the meantime. War, Conflict, Political Controversy, Diplomatic Talks, Social Exclusion, Financial Crisis.

This MUN Conference provides students with an opportunity to engage in extensive discussion and step into the position of skilled diplomats advocating for their beliefs and the ideals of our world. For our comeback conference, we provide 9 committees and enough material for all students to be able to accommodate themselves with international matters and the art of diplomacy.

We therefore urge you to participate in the 4th AMLMUN Conference and take advantage of the opportunity to use your voice and advocate for the right!

FAQ

Where is the conference?

The conference will be held at Anavryta Model Lyceum in Marousi, Athens.

When is the Form I Deadline?

Registration for form I closes on September 26, 2025.

When is the Form II Deadline?

Registration for form II closes on October 10, 2025.

What is the delegate fee?

The fees for the delegates participating in AML MUN are 30€ per delegate for the 2 days of the conference including lunch.
If any delegate does not want to receive the lunch provided by our Conference, the fees for those delegates are 5€ per delegate.
