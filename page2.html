4TH AML MUN TEAM

It is indeed, a true privilege and honour to invite you to our ‘Comeback’ AML MUN CONFERENCE! We have come a long way since our last MUN Conference as hosts, with our 3rd AML MUN Conference in 2020! We have grown, we have dealt with a number of challenges, we have endured loses! Yet, we have not lost our passion for MUN and our deep sense of commitment to this great universal cause! In this 4th Session of the AML MUN Conference, we aspire to bring together students from diverse educational backgrounds, with zeal to delve into international issues and act as Change Makers in their communities. It is also a unique opportunity to celebrate our school’s 75th Anniversary along with the 80th Anniversary of the United Nations!
Looking forward to seeing you at our beloved AML MUN Conference in November.

Evaggelia Karagianidou
AML MUN Director ELT Teacher, M.Ed. TESOL
